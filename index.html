Introduction

About Digital Scholar

India's First Agency-Style Digital Marketing Institute

Digital Scholar is India's first agency-style digital marketing institute, founded in Chennai in September 2019 by Sorav Jain and co-founded by Rishi Jain. What began as a bold experiment in redefining how digital marketing is taught has grown into one of India's most respected names in both education and corporate training.

The institute has evolved into one of the world's most advanced digital marketing institutions, with offline campuses in Chennai, Mumbai, Dubai, and a thriving global online presence.

Digital Scholar is built inside a live agency — echoVME Digital — that manages Rs 80+ lakhs in monthly ad spend. Students don't watch case studies. They work on the same campaigns running for Casagrand, Naturals Salon, and Bosch.

The Ecosystem Behind Digital Scholar

echoVME Digital — Founded 2011 by Sorav Jain

"Before Digital Scholar, there was echoVME. Understanding echoVME is key to understanding why Digital Scholar is built differently from every other institute in India."

— Sorav Jain, Founder

echoVME Digital is one of India's premier digital marketing agencies, founded in Chennai in 2011 by Sorav Jain. For over a decade, echoVME has delivered 360-degree digital services across real estate, FMCG, healthcare, and e-commerce, building lasting partnerships and custom roadmaps for every client.

Under Sorav's leadership, the agency houses 150+ marketers and has served 300+ brands including Amazon, Meta India, Bosch, Ramco, and Casagrand. This live agency is the core of Digital Scholar—serving as the training ground where students work on real budgets, real clients, and live campaigns.
